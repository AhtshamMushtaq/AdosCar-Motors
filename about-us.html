About Us

OUR GOAL AND MISSION

At Adoscar Motors Express, LLC, we have years of logistics experience, and aim to deliver your cargo in secured and timely manners in every part of the country. Whether you are moving one box or 100 boxes, we have the capability to provide interstate and intrastate logistics support for you. We emphasize safety and compliance as we earn your trust and business. We help you achieve timely and expedited pickups and deliveries, saving you time and money. This is evidenced by the various shippers whose average load capacities we have helped to increase. Basically, we have the capability to provide you with the most suitable vehicles to cover your Local and Over the Road (OTR) cargo deliveries.

Our Dump Truck division showcase Quads, Tri-Axles, and Tandem dump trucks that are engineered to reliably fit the needs of any desired region. This division is prepared to provide you with reliable Dump Trucks during your projects in the South and North East of the U.S.

We look forward to earning your trust and business. Please call 770-203-6241 for immediate bookings.
